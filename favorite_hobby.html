Favorite Hobby: Make All Things

My favorite hobby is to make all things. I heard the term when I worked at a company called Aleph Objects and we manufactured 3D printers. We just started an internal campaign called "Make All Things" and I liked it a whole lot! I learned so many skills and I realized that I just like to make all things. By just making all sorts of things I learned new skills from 3D modeling to video editing, programming and what ever I needed the skills for to do something.

Linker Lures

Something I did in 2019 after Aleph Objects went out of business and I lost my job was I worked on a YouTube channel of my own called Linker Lures. I designed my own fishing lures and 3D printed them. I tried them out and I wasn't too successful at catching fish with them but I had fun. I used skills I had to make the 3D model in a program called Blender. Then I learned some video editing skills and edited the video in Blender also. Here are my videos I posted to YouTube before I became too busy with school and other things and gave up on that project.
